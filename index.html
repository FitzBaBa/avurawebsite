Our Story

Avura Cares was founded with a simple goal — to change the way nigerian experience healthcare, Health for all. We started with a bold belief: healthcare should be preventive, not reactive; personal, not transactional.

Born from frustration with hard-to-access hospitals, expensive treatments, and a lack of trust, we built a system where care starts with listening.

Whether it's a phone call, a home visit or a personalized care plan — we meet you where you are. Avura Cares isn't just a name. it's a promise
